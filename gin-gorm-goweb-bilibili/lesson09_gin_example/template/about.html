About Us

Welcome to Oxy Club

At Cricket Club, we live and breathe cricket. Our mission is to celebrate the gentleman's game and create a community where fans, players, and enthusiasts can unite to share their passion. Whether you're a seasoned cricketer, an emerging talent, or a dedicated fan, our club has something for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2000, Cricket Club was established with the goal of promoting cricket at all levels. What began as a small group of cricket enthusiasts has grown into a thriving community. We have hosted numerous events, tournaments, and training sessions that have helped nurture local talent and brought together cricket lovers from all walks of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to foster a love for cricket and provide opportunities for everyone to engage with the sport. We believe in the power of cricket to bring people together, promote healthy lifestyles, and teach important life skills such as teamwork, discipline, and perseverance.
